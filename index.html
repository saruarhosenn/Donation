    Recent Charity Work and Future Plan

    Our aim for 2020

    • Build brand new homes for 100 families

    • Continue with our Masjid projects and build 5 brand new Masjid in 2020

    • Distribute 3000 family food packs (including in the month of Ramadan)

    • Install 300 Tube wells

    • Open new centres to empower women (Sewing machines & Computer courses)

    • Distribute 50 Rickshaws

    • Support at least 5 families which may have been afflicted by a serious medical condition & organize medical camps for cataract and health checkups

    • Continue educational support to blind students

    • Continue to provide Wheelchair distribution
